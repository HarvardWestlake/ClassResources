What you’ll explore
This activity is about how electric charge moves on objects:
  • Conduction: when conductors touch, net charge shares in proportion to size.
  • Induction: a nearby charged object can pull charges apart on a neutral conductor; grounding lets net charge flow.
What the colors mean
On conductors, the ring shows surface charge: red = positive, blue = negative. If everything is neutral, the ring appears white.
